About Profexional IT Group

Profexional IT Group is an entrepreneurial organization dedicated to building sustainable growth by acquiring, developing, and coordinating companies within the installation and technology sector. With a forward-looking mindset, we bring together diverse expertise and innovative solutions under one umbrella, creating synergies that deliver lasting value.

Our Core Services

At Profexional IT Group, we deliver innovative and future-ready solutions across multiple domains — from IT and infrastructure to digital transformation and security.

IT Solutions

Comprehensive IT services, from small-scale projects to enterprise-grade system development, integration, and software solutions.

Infrastructure

High-level expertise in digital infrastructure, networks, and cloud systems, designed for performance, scalability, and energy efficiency.

Security

Reliable cybersecurity and IT security solutions, focusing on protecting data, networks, and mission-critical digital assets.

Digital Transformation

Innovative solutions that help companies adapt to the digital era — including cloud migration, automation, and modern collaboration tools.

Benefits with Profexional IT Group

Through strong local presence, we build long-term client relationships and adapt solutions to meet regional market needs.
We provide central support functions such as IT, HR, and finance so that our companies can focus on growth and delivery.
With our own IT academy — both digital and physical — we raise the competence of employees and encourage knowledge sharing across teams.
We invest in safe, modern, and sustainable workplaces designed to foster collaboration and innovation.
By connecting with other companies within Profexional IT Group, employees gain access to broader expertise and new opportunities.
